## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Image Best Practices
1. **File Size**: Keep under 2MB for fast loading
2. **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
3. **Resolution**: High-quality but optimized
4. **Aesthetic**: Match BVCC's industrial, minimal vibe

### Content Writing
1. **Short & Punchy**: Keep text concise
2. **Brand Voice**: Professional, understated, sophisticated
3. **Mobile-First**: Many visitors browse on phones

### SEO Strategy
1. **Update Regularly**: Fresh content ranks better
2. **Use Keywords**: Include relevant terms naturally
3. **Meta Tags**: Always fill out title & description
4. **Social Sharing**: Upload OG image for better shares

### Performance
1. **Compress Images**: Use tools like TinyPNG
2. **Test Changes**: Click "Open website" after saving
3. **Clear Cache**: Hard refresh browser (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)

### Changes Not Appearing?
