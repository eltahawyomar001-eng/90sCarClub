About the Club

Before traction control.
Before drive modes.
Before algorithms.

There was a brief window when performance cars achieved mechanical perfection — powerful enough to thrill, analog enough to connect, refined enough to live with.

ANALOG is a private drivers' club dedicated to preserving and celebrating this golden era. We are not collectors. We are not investors. We are drivers who believe that the cars of 1990–2005 represent the purest expression of the automotive art form.

Our members share a common understanding: that a naturally aspirated engine at redline, a manual gearbox in third, and an unassisted steering rack on a mountain road is an experience worth protecting.

Membership

Join a community
of purists

ANALOG membership is by application only. We seek drivers who share our values — not the size of their collection, but the depth of their passion.

Requirements

  • Ownership of at least one qualifying vehicle (1990–2005)
  • Commitment to driving, not just displaying
  • Endorsement by an existing member (or compelling application)
  • Alignment with club values and community spirit

Member Benefits

  • Access to private driving events and road tours
  • Members-only track days at premier circuits
  • Curated mechanical workshop partnerships
  • Private marketplace for vetted vehicles
  • Quarterly publication and digital archive
